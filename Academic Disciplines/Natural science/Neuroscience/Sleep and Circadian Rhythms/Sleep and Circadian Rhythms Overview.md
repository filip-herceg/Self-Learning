# Sleep and Circadian Rhythms Overview

This field examines the neurobiological mechanisms of sleep, circadian rhythm regulation, and the effects of sleep and circadian disturbance on health and cognition.

## Topics

### Circadian Biology
- Circadian clock mechanisms
- Suprachiasmatic nucleus and entrainment
- Circadian regulation of physiology and behavior
- Individual differences in chronotype

### Sleep Stages and Architecture
- REM and non-REM sleep
- Sleep cycles and ultradian rhythms
- EEG characteristics of sleep stages
- Sleep across the lifespan

### Neurochemistry of Sleep
- Neurotransmitter systems regulating sleep-wake
- Hypocretin/orexin system
- Adenosine and sleep pressure
- Melatonin and circadian entrainment

### Sleep Functions
- Memory consolidation during sleep
- Metabolic and restorative functions
- Emotional regulation and sleep

### Sleep Disorders
- Insomnia mechanisms
- Narcolepsy and cataplexy
- Sleep apnea
- Parasomnia disorders

### Circadian Disorders
- Circadian misalignment
- Shift work and jet lag
- Seasonal affective disorder
