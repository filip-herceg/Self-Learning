# Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy Overview

Clinical psychology is the applied field focused on assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of mental health disorders and psychological problems. This section covers clinical assessment, psychotherapeutic approaches, psychiatric disorders, and biological treatments.

## Core Areas

- **Neuropsychology**: Assessment and rehabilitation of cognitive and behavioral problems due to brain injury or disease
- **Clinical Assessment and Diagnosis**: Psychological testing, interviewing, and diagnostic procedures
- **Psychotherapy Approaches**: Major therapeutic modalities and evidence-based practices
- **Psychiatric Disorders**: Classification, etiology, and symptoms of mental health disorders
- **Psychopharmacology**: Medications used in psychiatric treatment

## Related Fields

For foundational knowledge, see:
- **Psychology** (Social Science) - Core psychological theory and research
- **Neuroscience** (Natural Science) - Neurobiological bases
- **Educational Psychology** (Applied Science) - Prevention and early intervention
- **Forensic Psychology** (Applied Science) - Legal and ethical aspects
