# Clinical Assessment and Diagnosis Overview

Clinical assessment encompasses the collection and interpretation of psychological information to understand a client's presenting problems, guide treatment planning, and monitor progress.

## Topics

### Diagnostic Frameworks
- DSM-5 classification system
- ICD-11 diagnostic criteria
- Diagnostic interviews and procedures
- Differential diagnosis

### Psychological Testing
- Intelligence testing and cognitive assessment
- Personality testing (self-report and projective)
- Symptom rating scales
- Behavioral assessment

### Interview Techniques
- Structured and semi-structured interviews
- Motivational interviewing
- Assessment for suicidality and danger
- Mental status examination

### Assessment Domains
- Cognitive functioning
- Emotional and mood assessment
- Behavioral observation and analysis
- Social and occupational functioning
- Substance use assessment
- Trauma assessment

### Special Populations
- Assessment of children and adolescents
- Geriatric assessment
- Cultural considerations in assessment
- Assessment across diverse populations

### Report Writing and Communication
- Psychological report writing
- Case conceptualization
- Treatment recommendations
- Communication with clients and referral sources
