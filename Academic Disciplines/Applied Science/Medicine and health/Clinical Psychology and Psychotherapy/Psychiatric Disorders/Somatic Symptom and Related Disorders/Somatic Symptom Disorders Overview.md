# Somatic Symptom and Related Disorders Overview

These disorders feature prominent somatic symptoms or health concerns with disproportionate thoughts, feelings, or behaviors.

## Major Conditions
- Somatic symptom disorder
- Illness anxiety disorder
- Conversion (functional neurological) disorder
- Psychological factors affecting medical conditions
- Factitious disorder (imposed on self or another)

## Assessment
- Symptom characterization and medical evaluation
- Health anxiety and preoccupation
- Functional neurologic symptoms
- Care-seeking behavior and provider use

## Etiology
- Somatosensory amplification
- Health anxiety and catastrophic misinterpretation
- Trauma and stress
- Reinforcement and attention

## Treatment
- Psychoeducation and collaborative care
- CBT for health anxiety
- Gradual return to function
- Physiotherapy for functional symptoms
- Mindfulness/acceptance approaches
