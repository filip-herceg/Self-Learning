# Bipolar and Related Disorders Overview

Bipolar disorders involve episodic mood disturbances with manic/hypomanic and depressive episodes.

## Major Conditions
- Bipolar I disorder (mania +/- depression)
- Bipolar II disorder (hypomania + depression)
- Cyclothymic disorder
- Substance/medication-induced bipolar disorder
- Other specified/unspecified bipolar disorders

## Assessment
- Episode history (mania/hypomania/depression)
- Sleep, energy, activity changes
- Risk behaviors and impulsivity
- Suicide risk
- Rule out substances and medical causes

## Etiology
- Strong genetic component
- Circadian rhythm dysregulation
- Neurobiological factors (reward circuitry)
- Psychosocial stressors

## Treatment
- Mood stabilizers (lithium, valproate, lamotrigine)
- Atypical antipsychotics
- Psychoeducation and relapse prevention
- CBT, FFT, IPSRT
- Careful antidepressant use (risk of switching)

## Comorbidity
- Anxiety and substance use disorders
- ADHD and sleep disorders
