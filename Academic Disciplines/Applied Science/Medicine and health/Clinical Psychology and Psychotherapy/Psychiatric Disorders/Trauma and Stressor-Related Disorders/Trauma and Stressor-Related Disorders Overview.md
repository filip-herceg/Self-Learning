# Trauma and Stressor-Related Disorders Overview

These disorders are precipitated by exposure to traumatic or stressful events and involve characteristic symptom clusters and functional impairment.

## Major Conditions
- Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
- Acute stress disorder
- Adjustment disorders
- Reactive attachment disorder
- Disinhibited social engagement disorder

## Assessment
- Trauma history and exposure type
- Symptom clusters: intrusion, avoidance, negative mood/cognition, arousal
- Dissociation screening
- Functional impact and safety

## Etiology and Risk
- Severity and proximity of trauma
- Prior trauma and adversity
- Social support and resilience factors
- Biological stress systems (HPA axis)

## Treatment
- Trauma-focused CBT (TF-CBT)
- Prolonged exposure (PE) and cognitive processing therapy (CPT)
- EMDR
- Stress inoculation and grounding skills
- Pharmacotherapy (SSRIs first-line)

## Special Populations
- Children and adolescents
- Complex/developmental trauma
- First responders and veterans
