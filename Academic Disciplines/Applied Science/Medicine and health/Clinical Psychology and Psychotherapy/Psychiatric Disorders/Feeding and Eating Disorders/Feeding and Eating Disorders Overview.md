# Feeding and Eating Disorders Overview

These disorders involve persistent disturbance of eating or related behaviors leading to altered consumption/absorption and significant health or psychosocial impact.

## Major Conditions
- Anorexia nervosa
- Bulimia nervosa
- Binge-eating disorder
- Avoidant/restrictive food intake disorder (ARFID)
- Pica
- Rumination disorder

## Assessment
- Eating behaviors and compensatory methods
- Weight/BMI trends and medical risk
- Body image disturbance
- Menstrual and endocrine status
- Risk: electrolytes, cardiac concerns

## Etiology
- Genetic and temperamental factors
- Sociocultural pressures
- Cognitive distortions about shape/weight
- Trauma and perfectionism

## Treatment
- CBT-E; interpersonal psychotherapy (bulimia/binge-eating)
- Family-based treatment (FBT) for adolescents
- Nutritional rehabilitation and medical monitoring
- Pharmacotherapy (SSRIs for bulimia; lisdexamfetamine for BED)
