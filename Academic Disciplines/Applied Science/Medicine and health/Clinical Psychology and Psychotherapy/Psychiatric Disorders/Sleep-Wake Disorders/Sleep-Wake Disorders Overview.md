# Sleep-Wake Disorders Overview

Sleep-wake disorders involve disturbances in sleep quantity, quality, timing, or behaviors during sleep with daytime impairment.

## Major Conditions
- Insomnia disorder
- Hypersomnolence disorder
- Narcolepsy
- Breathing-related sleep disorders (obstructive/central sleep apnea)
- Circadian rhythm sleep-wake disorders
- Parasomnias (NREM disorders of arousal, nightmares, REM behavior disorder)
- Restless legs syndrome

## Assessment
- Sleep history and diaries
- Polysomnography, actigraphy
- Screening for medical/psychiatric contributors
- Substance/caffeine assessment

## Treatment
- CBT-I (first-line for insomnia)
- Sleep hygiene and stimulus control
- CPAP for sleep apnea
- Pharmacotherapy (hypnotics, wake-promoting agents)
- Chronotherapy/light therapy for circadian issues
- Safety planning for parasomnias
