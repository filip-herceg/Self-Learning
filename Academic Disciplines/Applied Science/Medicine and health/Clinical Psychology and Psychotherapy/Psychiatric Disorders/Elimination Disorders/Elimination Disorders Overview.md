# Elimination Disorders Overview

Elimination disorders involve inappropriate elimination of urine or feces, typically diagnosed in childhood.

## Major Conditions
- Enuresis (nocturnal, diurnal, or both)
- Encopresis (with/without constipation and overflow incontinence)

## Assessment
- Developmental level and toilet training history
- Medical evaluation (UTIs, constipation)
- Frequency and contexts
- Psychosocial stressors

## Treatment
- Behavioral interventions (alarms for enuresis)
- Scheduled toileting and reinforcement
- Treat constipation (encopresis)
- Psychoeducation and family support
- Desmopressin for enuresis (selected cases)
