# Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders Overview

These disorders involve intrusive thoughts/urges and repetitive behaviors or mental acts aimed at reducing distress, plus related body-focused behaviors.

## Major Conditions
- Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)
- Body dysmorphic disorder
- Hoarding disorder
- Trichotillomania (hair-pulling)
- Excoriation (skin-picking)

## Assessment
- Obsession/compulsion characterization
- Y-BOCS and related scales
- Functional impairment and safety
- Insight levels

## Etiology
- Genetic and neurobiological factors (cortico-striatal circuits)
- Learning and negative reinforcement
- Cognitive appraisals (inflated responsibility, thought-action fusion)

## Treatment
- Exposure and response prevention (ERP)
- Cognitive therapy for maladaptive beliefs
- SSRIs (often higher dose) and clomipramine
- Habit reversal training (trichotillomania, excoriation)
- Motivational strategies for hoarding

## Comorbidity
- Anxiety and depressive disorders
- Tics and Tourette
