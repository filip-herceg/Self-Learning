# Gender Dysphoria Overview

Gender dysphoria refers to distress associated with incongruence between experienced/expressed gender and assigned sex.

## Populations
- Children
- Adolescents
- Adults

## Assessment
- Duration and persistence of incongruence
- Impact on functioning and distress
- Co-occurring mental health concerns
- Social and family context

## Care Principles
- Gender-affirming, person-centered approach
- Exploration without pathologizing identity
- Informed consent models

## Interventions
- Psychotherapy (supportive, affirmative)
- Social transition support
- Voice and communication therapy

- Surgical options (per standards of care)

## Standards and Ethics
- WPATH Standards of Care
- Cultural sensitivity and minority stress
- Access and informed consent
