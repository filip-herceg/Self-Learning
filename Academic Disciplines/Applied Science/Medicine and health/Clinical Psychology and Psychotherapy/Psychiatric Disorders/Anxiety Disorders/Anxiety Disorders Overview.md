# Anxiety Disorders Overview

Anxiety disorders involve excessive fear or anxiety with behavioral and physiological correlates that cause distress or impairment.

## Major Conditions
- Generalized anxiety disorder
- Panic disorder
- Agoraphobia
- Social anxiety disorder
- Specific phobias
- Separation anxiety disorder
- Selective mutism

## Assessment
- Clinical interview and functional analysis
- Panic and anxiety rating scales
- Fear hierarchies
- Rule-outs (medical, substance-related)

## Etiology
- Genetic vulnerability
- Conditioning and learning
- Cognitive biases and threat appraisal
- Temperament (behavioral inhibition)

## Treatment
- CBT with exposure (in vivo, interoceptive)
- Cognitive restructuring
- Relaxation and breathing training
- SSRIs/SNRIs; benzodiazepines (short-term, with caution)
- Mindfulness-based interventions

## Comorbidity
- Depressive disorders
- Substance use
- Other anxiety disorders
