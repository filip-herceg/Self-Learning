# Psychotherapy Approaches Overview

Psychotherapy encompasses various evidence-based methods for treating psychological problems and promoting mental health. These approaches differ in theoretical foundations, techniques, and applications.

## Major Therapeutic Approaches

### Cognitive-Behavioral and Behavioral Approaches
- **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)**: Focus on relationships between thoughts, feelings, and behaviors
- **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)**: Combines CBT with dialectical philosophy for emotion dysregulation
- **Behavioral Therapy**: Emphasis on conditioning and learning principles
- **Schema Therapy**: Addresses deeply-rooted early maladaptive schemas and coping modes

### Acceptance and Process-Based Approaches
- **Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)**: Psychological flexibility through acceptance and values-based action
- **Mindfulness-Based Approaches**: Integration of meditation with therapeutic interventions (MBSR, MBCT, MBRP)
- **Somatic and Body-Based Therapies**: Using bodily awareness and movement for healing
- **Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT)**: Goal-directed focus on strengths and preferred futures

### Relational and Process-Focused Approaches
- **Psychodynamic Therapy**: Emphasis on unconscious processes and early relationships
- **Humanistic Therapies**: Client-centered and existential approaches emphasizing personal growth
- **Attachment-Based Therapies**: Using therapeutic relationship as corrective experience (EFT, PCIT, DDP)
- **Mentalization-Based Therapy (MBT)**: Enhancing capacity to understand mental states
- **Family Systems Therapy**: Understanding individuals within relational and family context

### Trauma and Specialized Approaches
- **EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)**: Using bilateral stimulation for trauma processing
- **Interpersonal Therapy (IPT)**: Structured approach addressing depression through interpersonal improvement
- **Motivational Interviewing (MI)**: Evoking intrinsic motivation for behavior change
- **Creative Therapies**: Art, music, drama, dance/movement, and play therapy
