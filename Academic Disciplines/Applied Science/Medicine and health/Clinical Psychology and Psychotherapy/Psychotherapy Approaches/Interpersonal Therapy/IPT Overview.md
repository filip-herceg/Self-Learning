# Interpersonal Therapy Overview

Interpersonal Therapy (IPT) is a structured, time-limited psychotherapy addressing depression and other mood disorders through improvement of interpersonal relationships and social functioning.

## Theoretical Foundations

- Attachment theory
- Social role theory
- Communication and social psychology
- Interpersonal-behavioral framework

## Theoretical Model

- Links between mood and interpersonal factors
- Four problem areas: grief, interpersonal role disputes, role transitions, interpersonal deficits
- Communication analysis
- Social functioning

## Therapy Structure

### Assessment Phase
- Interpersonal inventory
- Problem formulation
- Treatment contract
- Goals and expectations

### Middle Phase
- Focus on identified problem area
- Specific techniques for each problem area
- Behavioral change
- Communication improvement

### Termination Phase
- Consolidation of gains
- Independence and self-efficacy
- Prevention of relapse
- Future planning

## Problem-Specific Interventions

### Grief
- Emotional expression
- Exploration of loss
- Relationship reappraisal
- Finding new meanings

### Interpersonal Role Disputes
- Identification of dispute
- Communication analysis
- Negotiation and compromise
- Resolution strategies

### Role Transitions
- Exploration of loss and identity
- Development of new role
- Recognition of positive aspects
- Social skills development

### Interpersonal Deficits
- Social skills training
- Communication improvement
- Relationship building
- Loneliness reduction

## Applications

### Primary Application: Depression
- Major depressive disorder
- Adolescent depression
- Postpartum depression
- Chronic depression

### Other Disorders
- Bipolar disorder
- Anxiety disorders
- Eating disorders
- Substance use
- Personality disorders

### Special Populations
- Adolescents
- Elderly individuals
- Medical comorbidity
- Cross-cultural applications

## Research and Evidence

- Efficacy for depression
- Mechanisms of change
- Comparison with other therapies
- Long-term outcomes
