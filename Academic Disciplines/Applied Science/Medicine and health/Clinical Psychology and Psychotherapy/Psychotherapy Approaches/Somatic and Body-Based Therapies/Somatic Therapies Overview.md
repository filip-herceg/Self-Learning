# Somatic and Body-Based Therapies Overview

Somatic and body-based therapies recognize the mind-body connection, using bodily awareness, movement, and physical sensation as pathways to healing and psychological change.

## Theoretical Foundations

- Mind-body integration
- Trauma held in body
- Nervous system regulation
- Embodied cognition
- Sensorimotor processing

## Core Concepts

- Body wisdom and intelligence
- Somatic awareness and sensation
- Movement and expression
- Tension and holding patterns
- Nervous system states

## Major Approaches

### Somatic Experiencing (SE)
- Pendulation between resources and trauma
- Titration and gradual processing
- Completion of defensive responses
- Nervous system reprocessing

### Sensorimotor Psychotherapy
- Somatic-cognitive integration
- Movement patterns and trauma
- Body-based interventions
- Reflective integration

### Hakomi
- Mindful awareness and somatic focus
- Core material exploration
- Gentle and respectful approach
- Body-based insight

### Gestalt Somatic Therapy
- Body awareness and presence
- Authentic expression
- Organismic self-regulation
- Environmental contact

### Bioenergetic Analysis
- Energy flow and body armoring
- Muscular tension and blocks
- Breath and expression
- Integration and authenticity

### Trauma-Sensitive Yoga
- Yoga for trauma survivors
- Consent and autonomy
- Internal sensation focus
- Nervous system regulation

## Techniques and Practices

- Somatic awareness exercises
- Breathwork and breathing techniques
- Movement exploration
- Dance and expressive movement

- Body scanning

- Pendulation between states
