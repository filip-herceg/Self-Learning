# Educational Psychology Overview

Educational psychology applies psychological science to learning and teaching contexts, examining how students learn, how teachers can facilitate learning, and how educational systems can support development.

## Core Topics

### Learning Theories
- Behavioral approaches to learning
- Cognitive learning theories
- Social-cognitive theory
- Constructivism and situated learning
- Deep versus surface learning

### Instruction and Teaching
- Instructional design principles
- Effective teaching strategies
- Classroom management
- Differentiated instruction
- Universal Design for Learning

### Motivation and Learning
- Intrinsic and extrinsic motivation
- Goal orientation
- Self-efficacy
- Engagement and persistence
- Anxiety and test performance

### Development and Learning
- Cognitive development and learning
- Emotional development in school
- Social development and peer relationships
- Identity development

### Individual Differences
- Learning styles and preferences
- Intelligence and ability
- Personality and learning
- Multicultural considerations

### Special Education
- Identification and assessment
- Learning disabilities
- Intellectual disabilities
- Gifted education
- Behavioral and emotional disorders

### Technology and Learning
- Digital learning and online education
- Adaptive learning systems
- Computer-assisted instruction
- Educational games and simulations

### School-Based Mental Health
- Prevention and intervention programs
- School counseling
- Crisis response
- Bullying and social-emotional learning

## Research and Practice

- Evidence-based practices
- Outcomes assessment
- Program evaluation
- School effectiveness research
