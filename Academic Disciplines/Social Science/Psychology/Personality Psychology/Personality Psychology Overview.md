# Personality Psychology Overview

Personality psychology examines stable patterns of thoughts, feelings, and behaviors that characterize individuals and how these patterns emerge and change.

## Topics

### Personality Theories
- Psychoanalytic theories (Freud, Jung, Adler, Erikson)
- Humanistic theories (Rogers, Maslow)
- Behavioral and social-cognitive theories
- Trait theories
- Type theories

### Personality Traits
- Big Five personality factors
- Factor analysis and trait measurement
- Cross-cultural personality traits
- Trait stability and change

### Personality Assessment
- Self-report inventories
- Projective tests
- Observer ratings
- Behavioral assessment

### Individual Differences
- Temperament
- Emotional reactivity
- Cognitive style
- Coping styles

### Applications
- Personality and relationships
- Personality and work performance
- Personality and mental health
- Personality and creativity
