# Cross-Cultural Psychology Overview

Cross-cultural psychology examines how cultural factors shape human behavior, cognition, and development, investigating psychological phenomena across diverse cultural contexts and exploring cultural universals and differences.

- Cultural dimensions and frameworks
- Individualism-collectivism

- Cultural psychology vs. cultural comparative psychology
