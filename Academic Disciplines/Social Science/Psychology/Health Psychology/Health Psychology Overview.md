# Health Psychology Overview

Health psychology examines psychological and behavioral factors in physical health, illness, and healthcare, investigating how psychology influences health outcomes.

## Topics

### Stress and Coping
- Stress physiology and the stress response
- Coping mechanisms and strategies
- Post-traumatic stress
- Resilience and hardiness

### Health Behaviors
- Health behavior models and change
- Smoking and tobacco use
- Alcohol and substance use
- Exercise and physical activity
- Diet and nutrition

### Pain and Chronic Illness
- Acute and chronic pain
- Pain management strategies
- Living with chronic illness
- Adjustment to illness

### Psychosocial Factors in Disease
- Cardiovascular disease and psychology
- Cancer and psychosocial factors
- Immune function and psychological factors
- Sleep and health

### Health Communication
- Doctor-patient communication
- Health literacy
- Adherence to medical regimens
- Health education

### Mental Health and Physical Health
- Depression and physical health
- Anxiety and somatic symptoms
- Mindfulness and health
- Mind-body medicine
