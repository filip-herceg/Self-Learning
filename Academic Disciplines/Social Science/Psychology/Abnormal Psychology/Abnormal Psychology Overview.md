# Abnormal Psychology Overview

Abnormal psychology examines maladaptive thoughts, feelings, and behaviors that cause distress and impairment, examining psychological disorders and their etiology.

## Topics

### Anxiety Disorders
- Generalized anxiety disorder
- Panic disorder
- Specific phobias
- Social anxiety disorder
- Agoraphobia

### Mood Disorders
- Major depression
- Bipolar disorder
- Persistent depressive disorder
- Seasonal affective disorder

### Trauma and Stress-Related Disorders
- Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
- Acute stress disorder
- Adjustment disorders

### Dissociative Disorders
- Dissociative identity disorder
- Depersonalization/derealization disorder
- Other specified dissociative disorder

### Substance Use Disorders
- Addiction and dependence
- Substance abuse
- Withdrawal and tolerance

### Psychotic Disorders
- Schizophrenia
- Delusional disorder
- Brief psychotic disorder

### Other Disorders
- Personality disorders
- Eating disorders
- Sleep disorders
- Obsessive-compulsive and related disorders

### Etiology and Models
- Biological models and genetics
- Psychological models
- Social and environmental factors
- Diathesis-stress model
