# Social Psychology Overview

Social psychology examines how people think about, influence, and relate to one another within social contexts and groups.

## Topics

### Attitudes and Persuasion
- Attitude formation and change
- Attitude-behavior relationship
- Persuasion principles and resistance
- Cognitive dissonance

### Conformity and Influence
- Conformity and obedience
- Compliance techniques
- Group influence and social pressure
- Minority influence

### Group Behavior
- Group cohesion and performance
- Leadership
- Intergroup conflict and cooperation
- Social identity theory

### Attraction and Relationships
- Interpersonal attraction
- Love and attachment in relationships
- Relationship maintenance and dissolution
- Evolutionary perspectives

### Aggression and Prosocial Behavior
- Aggression: biological, social, and situational factors
- Media violence and aggression
- Helping behavior and altruism
- Empathy and compassion

### Attribution and Judgment
- Causal attribution
- Attribution biases
- Heuristics and biases in judgment
- Stereotyping and prejudice
