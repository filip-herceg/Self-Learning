# Community Psychology Overview

Community psychology focuses on understanding individuals within their community contexts and developing preventive interventions and social change to enhance community well-being and social justice.

## Core Values and Principles

- Community competence and empowerment
- Prevention and early intervention
- Social justice and advocacy
- Collaboration and partnership
- Cultural sensitivity and diversity
