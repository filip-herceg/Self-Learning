# Evolutionary Psychology Overview

Evolutionary psychology investigates how psychological mechanisms evolved through natural selection, examining the adaptive functions of behavior, cognition, and emotion in ancestral environments.

## Theoretical Foundations

- Evolutionary theory and natural selection
- Adaptation and design features
- Inclusive fitness and kin selection
- Reciprocal altruism
- Sexual selection

- Reproductive success and fitness

- Family dynamics
- Sibling relationships
- Parental care

### Social Behavior
- Cooperation and conflict
- Status and dominance

- Altruism and helping
- Cheating detection and cooperation

### Emotions and Motivation

- Life history strategies
