# Behavioral Psychology Overview

Behavioral psychology focuses on understanding how organisms learn through interaction with their environment and how past experiences shape behavior.

## Topics

### Classical Conditioning
- Pavlovian conditioning
- Acquisition and extinction
- Stimulus generalization and discrimination
- Conditioned emotional responses

### Operant Conditioning
- Reinforcement and punishment
- Schedules of reinforcement
- Shaping and chaining
- Behavior modification

### Learning Theory
- Associative learning
- Latent learning
- Observational learning
- Biological constraints on learning

### Applied Behavior Analysis
- Functional behavior assessment
- Behavioral interventions
- Token economies
- Contingency management

## Historical Context

This field emerged from the work of Pavlov, Thorndike, Skinner, and others who sought to understand learning through objective, observable behavior.
